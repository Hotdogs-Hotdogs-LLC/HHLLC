SUNDAYS AFTER CHURCH

"Sundays After Church" is a comedic web series set in South Florida, starring Zach Mangold and Erik Kris. Join this dynamic duo as they take you on a laughter-filled journey through their weekly misadventures. With a mix of hilarious skits and witty banter, each episode of this captivating comedy series promises to leave you entertained and eagerly waiting for the next installment. Tune in every Sunday to brighten your day with the comedic brilliance of "Sundays After Church."
