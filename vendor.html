Job Title:

Hotdog Cart Vendor

Job Description:

We are looking for two friendly and outgoing sales associates to join our company. The ideal candidate will have excellent customer service skills, be comfortable working in an outdoor environment, and be willing to wear a bikini while working.

Responsibilities:

  • Sell hotdogs to customers
  • Handle cash transactions
  • Maintain a clean and organized workspace
  • Ensure customer satisfaction by providing excellent service
  • Adhere to health and safety regulations
  • Drive vehicle towing the hotdog stand

Qualifications:

  • High school diploma or equivalent
  • Ability to interact with customers in a friendly and professional manner
  • Comfortable working in a potentially hot and outdoor environment
  • Willingness to wear a bikini while working
  • Availability to work on weekends or during special events

Compensation:

Employees will be compensated with 20-25% of the daily profits each as well as customer gratuity payments.
